Life and Upcomings

When I was growing up there was always something that i would want and wouldn't stop to get it whether it be a long term goal or something simple that I could accomplish any other day. The way I like to think is if someone else is able to do something then why wouldn't I be able do it? Of course to me the answer was obvious that I can do it. My mother has always been a big motivation in my life because she supports the idea that I can do anything very heavily. I hate to fall short of anyones expectations of me so I like to think that I try my hardest with any tasks I am given. I am very thankful to have my family as a source of motivation because it has always pushed me to go beyond my own expectations of myself.
